How to Play

You're a time traveler with knowledge of the future. Use it to trade stocks and build your fortune - but don't attract too much attention from the SEC.

📈 Buy & Sell: Select a stock from the left panel, enter a dollar amount, and click Buy. Close positions from the Positions panel.
🎯 Quarterly Targets: Hit net worth milestones each quarter or get fired. Early completion gives bonus time for the next target.
🔍 SEC Attention: Trading too well raises suspicion. If SEC Attention hits 100%, you get arrested and the run ends.
⏱️ Speed Controls: Use speed buttons (0.5x - 50x) or press Space to pause. Each tick = 1 trading day.
🏆 Points: Earned by hitting quarterly targets. Spend them in the Shop to unlock upgrades for future runs.
